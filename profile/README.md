## 🏭 Who We Are

**Copamate** is an Australian-owned, AS9100, IRIS, and ISO 9001 certified custom engineering solutions company based in Campbellfield, Melbourne. With over **30 years of experience** and **4,000 m² of in-house manufacturing capabilities**, we deliver new build and sustainment manufacturing to some of Australia's most demanding industries.

We serve clients across:

| Industry | Industry | Industry |
|---|---|---|
| 🚃 Rail | 🛡️ Defence | 🔧 Pipeline |
| 🏗️ Infrastructure | 🚗 Automotive | 🔩 Valves & Steel Framing |

Our capabilities span metal fabrication, laser cutting, water jet cutting, CNC machining, precision machining, 3D printing, cold spray, painting & coating, jigs & fixtures, product assembly, and refurbishment.

## 🧭 Contributing

If you're a Copamate team member working on an internal tool:

1. **Create a new repo** under this organisation — don't use personal accounts for org tools
2. **Include a README** explaining what the tool does, how to set it up, and who to contact
3. **Use clear commit messages** — future you will thank present you
4. **Add a `.gitignore`** — never commit credentials, file paths, or sensitive data
5. **Tag releases** when a tool is deployed to production systems
